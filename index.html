Founded in 2008, RAJA RANI GROUP began its journey with a vision to transform the real estate landscape with honest practices and high-quality construction. What started as a modest venture has now evolved into a well-recognized real estate enterprise, driven by dedication, teamwork, and a deep understanding of customer needs.

Over the past 15+ years RAJA RANI GROUP has built a strong foundation in Odisha, particularly in the regions of Berhampur and beyond. The company's consistency in maintaining transparency and ensuring high construction standards has made us a household name among property seekers and investors.
